Credit Builder

Credit-building solutions for consumers and businesses, designed to help you qualify for funding and reduce future interest expenses.

+65 pts average increase across providers

We use a range of credit-building and repair tools to offer solutions tailored to each applicant, helping expedite the qualification process for better rates and terms.

Product Types

Installment Credit (Credit Builder Loan)

  • Works like a loan repaid gradually over time
  • Monthly payments are reported to all bureaus; once complete, the funds are refunded minus fees

Revolving Credit (Credit Builder Secure Card)

  • Functions like a credit card with monthly settlements
  • You deposit funds in advance to access a credit limit, which is reported to the bureaus

Bill & Rent Reporting

  • Adds fresh on-time payment history
  • Connect recurring bills, including rent, for guaranteed bureau reporting

Credit Repair

  • Removes inaccurate details from your credit reports
  • Uses AI and automation to contest errors with the bureaus

How It Works

  • Request a complimentary consultation, or we'll proactively reach out to you
  • Our team helps you navigate and apply for the solutions that fit your situation
  • We continuously track score improvements and reassess eligibility for further opportunities
