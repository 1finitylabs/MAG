Small Business Health Insurance

Specialized health coverage for self-employed individuals, business owners, employees and their families.

The health of management and staff directly impacts the success of a small business. That's why MAG has teamed up with Senior Licensed Agent Andrew Warner to provide specialized health insurance solutions built for small businesses — not just larger volume clients.

Traditional broker options tend to favor larger groups, and public exchanges can carry high deductibles and coinsurance even with subsidies. The private market offers nationwide coverage at lower prices, tailored to individuals and families rather than requiring large group participation.
