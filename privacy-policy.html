Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Effective as of January 31, 2023

This policy applies to magbusinesscapital.com and Milestone Advisory Group LLC ("MAG Business Capital"), and explains how we collect and use information. By using our website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

To provide our products and services, we may collect identifying details such as your name, mailing address, email address and phone number. If you purchase a product or service, we also collect the billing information needed to complete that transaction. We only collect what you choose to share with us — for example, when you register for an account, request an offer, or send us a message.

How We Use It

We use your information to deliver the services you've requested and to let you know about other products or services from MAG Business Capital and our affiliates that may be relevant to you.

Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell, rent or lease your information to third parties. We may occasionally share your contact details with trusted partners to help with statistical analysis, communications, customer support, or to fulfill a specific offer you've expressed interest in. Any partner we share data with is required to keep it confidential and use it only to provide that service. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, property or safety of MAG Business Capital or the public.

Your Right to Opt Out

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar laws, you have the right to know what categories of personal information we've disclosed for a business purpose, and to opt out of that disclosure. To opt out, contact us at info@magbusinesscapital.com.

Security

We use SSL encryption to protect sensitive information such as payment details when it's transmitted through our site. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Right to Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal information from our records, and that we direct our service providers to do the same — subject to certain exceptions, such as completing a transaction already in progress, complying with a legal obligation, or detecting and preventing fraud.

Children Under Thirteen

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under thirteen. If you are under thirteen, please ask a parent or guardian for permission before using this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated by email or a notice on our website. Continued use of the site after an update means you accept the revised policy.
