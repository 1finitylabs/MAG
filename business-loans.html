Business Loans For Every Stage Of Growth

Whatever your current business needs, MAG Business Capital offers a wide range of financing options through our network of banks, lenders and strategic partners.

With additional capital, you'll have the opportunity to fund the initiatives that move your business forward — and once those investments are made, you'll start to see the long-term impact quickly. MAG Business Capital connects you with several funders so you can compare offers and pick the solution that actually fits your business.

Loan Programs We Offer

Revenue-Based Financing

A lender purchases a portion of your future revenue at a discount; you repay it with fixed daily or weekly payments.

Business Line of Credit

Borrow up to a set credit limit and only pay interest on the funds you actually draw each month.

Business Term Loan

A lump sum paid back over time with regular, scheduled payments based on your credit profile.

Invoice Factoring

Sell outstanding invoices to a lender who advances you a portion upfront, with the remainder released once your customer pays.

Equipment Financing

Get the cash you need to purchase the equipment your business depends on.

SBA Financing

Streamline SBA, SBA7A and SBA504 programs, plus asset-based financing, debt consolidation and purchase order financing.
